# Support

Community source is provided under its applicable MIT or MPL-2.0 license without support or warranty.

Fluxy Official customers receive only the support, maintenance access, response targets, warranty, or service levels stated in their signed order and support terms. Unless a contract says otherwise:

- GitHub issues are not an emergency support channel.
- No response or resolution time is guaranteed.
- Maintenance expiration ends access to new entitled official releases and support.
- Previously entitled versions remain operational and retain their open-source rights.

Inductive Automation support does not support third-party Fluxy code unless Inductive Automation separately agrees.
