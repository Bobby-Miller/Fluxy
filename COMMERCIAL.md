# Fluxy Official Commercial Model

Fluxy Official is a subscription for official release services, not a purchase of permission to use MPL-2.0 source.

Subject to the applicable order and contract, the subscription may provide:

- A Green Pipe Partners code-signed `.modl`.
- Native Ignition activation for authorized Gateways.
- Trusted release provenance, signed checksums, SBOMs, and source tags.
- Access to official versions released during the maintenance term.
- Support, security updates, installation, and upgrade convenience.
- Warranty or service levels only when expressly contracted.

When maintenance expires, previously entitled versions are intended to continue running. New official versions and support require renewal. Activation credentials remain valid for entitled versions, subject to agreed Gateway transfer and misuse rules. Customers retain all MIT and MPL rights to source already released.

MPL users may modify activation checks, compile and sign forks, and redistribute those forks under MPL-2.0. They do not receive Green Pipe Partners' certificate, activation service, update channel, support, warranties, or right to call a fork an official Fluxy release.

Commercial contracts should define per-Gateway activation, redundant Gateway treatment, transfers, support hours, response targets, exclusions, credential misuse, and trademark terms without restricting MPL modification or redistribution rights. Counsel must approve those terms before sale.
