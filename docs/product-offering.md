# Fluxy Official Website Content

This document is the publishable content plan for the product website. Pricing, checkout, support contacts, signed downloads, and legal terms remain launch blockers and must not be invented in repository copy.

## Product Position

Fluxy connects external Python automation to an allowlisted set of Ignition Gateway operations. The Python client is MIT licensed. The Ignition Gateway module is MPL-2.0.

Fluxy Official provides Green Pipe Partners code-signed modules, native Ignition activation, trusted provenance, official updates released during maintenance, installation convenience, and contracted support. Customers do not purchase permission to use MPL source.

## Required Pages

- Product overview and supported Ignition/Python versions.
- MIT/MPL licensing explanation linked to public source and `LICENSING.md`.
- Fluxy Official subscription benefits and per-Gateway activation model.
- Published pricing or a sales-contact path approved by Green Pipe.
- Signed trial module that operates during the Ignition trial.
- Immutable source tag/archive, signed checksum, SBOM, and provenance for every module download.
- Installation, activation, offline activation, upgrade, transfer, redundancy, and uninstall documentation.
- Maintenance and support terms, hours, response targets, and exclusions.
- Security reporting and release-advisory pages.
- Trademark policy and clear labeling of official versus community builds.

## Required Statements

- Previously entitled versions continue operating after maintenance expires.
- Maintenance renewal provides newer entitled official versions and support.
- MPL users may modify, compile, sign, and redistribute compliant forks.
- Forks do not receive Green Pipe's certificate, activation service, official update channel, support, warranties, or official-release identity.
- Fluxy is a third-party product. Do not imply Inductive Automation endorsement, certification, or support.

## Launch Blockers

- Counsel-approved subscription, support, CLA, privacy, and trademark terms.
- IA Module Showcase onboarding, module ID/vendor ID, and licensing access.
- CA-issued code-signing certificate and controlled signing infrastructure.
- Approved prices, checkout/sales workflow, tax handling, and support channel.
- First immutable source release and signed trial artifact.
