# Contributor Agreement Policy

**Status: blocked pending counsel approval and an operational signature system.**

Green Pipe Partners, LLC requires a company CLA before accepting external contributions. The purpose is to preserve contributors' rights while granting the company enough copyright and patent authority to distribute Fluxy under MIT, MPL-2.0, or future licenses and to offer official commercial releases.

## Required Records

- Legal name and verified contact information.
- Individual CLA execution date and agreement version.
- Corporate CLA and authorized signatory when the contribution is owned by an employer or other entity.
- GitHub identities covered by the agreement.
- Internal agreement reference and revocation/termination notes, if applicable.

Do not store signatures, home addresses, or other sensitive agreement records in the public repository. A pull request check should query the approved internal CLA registry before merge.

The draft forms in `cla/` are intentionally marked for counsel review. They are not operational merely because they are committed.
